Nok Heads

Nok terracotta heads (500 BCE) show intricate hairstyles and facial scarification, representing some of the earliest sculptural traditions in sub-Saharan Africa.

Nok Figures

Full-bodied Nok figures often depict seated or kneeling poses, showcasing the culture's advanced understanding of human anatomy and proportion.

Nok Fragments

Fragments reveal technical details of Nok clay modeling, including their unique hollow construction method and firing techniques.
