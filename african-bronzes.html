Benin Bronzes

Benin bronzes (Nigeria, 16th century) depict royalty and court life, created using the lost-wax casting technique for the Kingdom of Benin.

Ife Bronzes

Ife bronzes (Nigeria, 12th–15th century) showcase naturalistic human figures, renowned for their sophisticated metalworking techniques.

Ritual Objects

Ceremonial objects used in rituals and ancestor worship, often featuring symbolic designs representing spiritual concepts.
