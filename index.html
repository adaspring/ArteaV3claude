About ARTEA

ARTEA is dedicated to showcasing and preserving the rich artistic heritage of Africa. Our collection features authentic pieces that represent various cultural traditions across the continent.

Founded in 2020, we aim to educate and inspire through our carefully curated exhibitions of traditional African art forms.
